Autonomous Operation
Three Pillars of Security
Complete end-to-end autonomous vulnerability lifecycle management
🔍
Threat Detection
Continuous scanning of package.json and lock files against the OSV.dev database and npm audit API. Identifies CVEs, CWEs, and supply chain risks in real-time.
📊
Threat Analysis
CVSS-based severity scoring, dependency graph visualization, and root cause analysis differentiating direct vs transitive vulnerabilities.
🔧
Autonomous Remediation
Auto-generates patched package.json, creates upgrade diffs, and produces remediation reports — all without human intervention.
📋
Audit Reports
Professional PDF-ready security reports with full CVE details, CVSS scores, affected version ranges, and compliance-ready documentation.
🔗
Dependency Graph
Visual mapping of your entire dependency tree, highlighting vulnerable packages, transitive risks, and safe upgrade paths.
⚙️
Zero Human Dependency
Fully autonomous pipeline — from detection to remediation. No manual steps, no human bottlenecks in the vulnerability management lifecycle.
